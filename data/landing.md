## 2. Why PepTrack exists

I built PepTrack because keeping track of a personal routine shouldn't mean a messy notes app, a half-used spreadsheet, and a memory you can't fully trust.

People organizing a peptide or GLP-1 routine are juggling a lot of small details — when something was logged, what the rotation looked like, how the week felt, where the numbers landed. Most of that lives scattered across screenshots and reminders. PepTrack puts it in one place, designed to be quiet and private, so the record is yours and the app gets out of your way.

PepTrack is a tracking, organization, reminder, and progress-journal tool. It is not a coach, not a clinician, and not a source of advice. It simply helps you keep a clear, private record.

---

## 3. Key features

- **Injection & dose logging** — record entries as you go and keep a clean, time-stamped history.
- **Reminders** — set custom reminders so the routine you've chosen stays organized.
- **Calculator** — a simple utility to help you do your own math and record the figures you enter.
- **Site rotation tracking** — log and visualize rotation so you can see your own pattern at a glance.
- **Progress journal** — note weight, how you're feeling, side effects, and observations over time.
- **Charts & history** — see your logged entries laid out clearly across days and weeks.
- **Export** — export your own data when you want a copy or want to share it with someone you trust.
- **Privacy by design** — local-first storage; your entries live on your device, not on our servers.

## 4. Who it helps

PepTrack is for people who are already organizing a peptide or GLP-1 routine and want one calm place to keep the record straight. That includes:

- People who want a single, private log instead of scattered notes and screenshots.
- People who like seeing their own history, rotation, and observations laid out clearly.
- People who want gentle reminders to keep their chosen routine organized.
- People who value privacy and want their data to stay on their device.

PepTrack doesn't promise outcomes or tell you what to do. It helps you stay organized and keep your own record.
